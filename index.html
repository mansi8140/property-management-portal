Margarita is a beautiful Island located in South America in the Caribbean Sea between latitudes 10°52'N and 11°11'N and longitudes 63°48'W and 64°23'W. It belongs to Venezuela:

  • Beaches: It has over 70 beaches with 106 miles of coastlines
  • Terrains: It has terrains that are 760 meters above sea level
  • Mountains: It has mountain ranges in the east with a peak height of 920 meters above sea level
  • Climate: The average temperature is 90 degrees Fahrenheit
